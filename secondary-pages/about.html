Team Blue

“BLUE” is a web application that allows users to monitor their mental well-being by taking their input, assessing the daily pattern, and presenting wellness recommendations based on results.

The web application stands to challenge the idea that blue is associated with negative emotions and encourages embracing and understanding all emotions, regardless of what they are.

The website stands to reinforce the importance of assessing ALL our different emotions, rather than pushing them to the back of our minds.
