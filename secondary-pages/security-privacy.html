Security & Privacy Policy

At Blue, we take the security and privacy of our users very seriously. Please take a look at our Security & Privacy Policy which outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information as you use Blue.

  • Data Collection
    • Personal Information: When you sign up for an account on Blue, your name, email address, and password is collected.
    • Emotion Tracking Data: Our website tracks your emotions over time to provide personalized wellness recommendations. This data is stored securely and used only to better your experience with Blue.
    • Journal Entries and Goals: Any journal entries and goals you input on our website are stored securely and privately.
  • Data Usage
    • Personalized Recommendations: The data collected from your emotion tracking is used to provide you with personalized wellness recommendations.
    • Improvement of Services: Your feedback helps improve our services and better understand what our users need.
  • Data Security
    • Security: All data is protected from unauthorized access.
    • Data Storage: Your data is stored securely on servers.
  • Data Sharing
    • Third Parties: Your personal information, emotion tracking data, journal entries, or goals will not be shared with third parties without your consent.
  • Compliance
    • Legal Compliance: We comply with all applicable laws and regulations regarding the collection, use, and protection of personal information.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Security & Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@blueapp.com.
